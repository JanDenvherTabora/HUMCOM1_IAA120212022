How to Cook Beef Shawarma Rice

Ingredients

Rice:

  • 5 cups of cooked rice(Equal to at least 2 cups of raw rice)
  • garlic (minced)
  • salt
  • pepper
  • turmeric powder

Beef:

  • 1 kilo of ground beef
  • 1 tablespoon of oyster sauce
  • 1 tablespoon of soy sauce
  • 1/2 tablespoon of sugar
  • garlic (minced)
  • salt
  • pepper

Toppings:

  • tomato (julienne cut)
  • cucumber (julienne cut)
  • white onion (julienne cut)

Intrucrtions

  1. In a pan at medium heat, put oil and wait until it's well-heated before adding the garlic. Saute until golden brown.
  2. When the garlic has that nice golden brown color, put your rice in the pan. Add a pinch of turmeric powder, salt, and pepper. Mix well.
  3. After the rice is mixed all the way, cover the pan, then set aside.
  4. In another pan, medium heat, put a little bit of oil, heat for 1-2 minutes before adding garlic, then your ground beef.
  5. If you see that the ground beef nearly cooked, add sugar, oyster sauce, soy sauce, and a pinch of salt and pepper to taste. Combine it nicely and let it cook for another 2-3 minutes. Remove the pan in the heat after a few minutes, then set aside.
  6. Serve it on a plate. Place your rice first, beef second, and top it with cucumber, tomato, and white onion.
